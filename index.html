Vincite Provincias

How to play

To play, enter the name of your first team in the textbox, then click "Add Team"
Continue the above step until you have entered the last team team
Type the team name into the textbox and click "Start Game"
To aquire a country, click on the title text until it changes to the color of your team.

Some History

One day, I happened to mention to my Latin teacher that I programmed, she asked me to make a digital version of a game we were playing that day in class, called "Vincite Provincias." I quickly wrote up a proof of concept in p5.js, and I have been fine tuning it ever since.
